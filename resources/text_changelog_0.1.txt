# Squire v0.1

Squire is a fork of Bobby v1.4.

## What's new

This is the first release of Squire! Squire connects you to AI assistants via Telegram, giving your Pebble agentic superpowers.

## Telegram Integration

Squire works by connecting to a Telegram bot connected to either Hermes or OpenClaw. Once connected, you can chat with your AI assistant right from your wrist.
